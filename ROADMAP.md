# Global Earnings Radar - Roadmap

## Product Vision

A financial research assistant for US and European tech stocks that helps users quickly understand earnings reports, business trends, and risk factors—without the complexity of reading full 10-K filings.

**Positioning**: Educational tool, not investment advisor.
